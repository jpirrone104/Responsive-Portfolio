About Me

Hello! My name is Jen, and I am an aspiring Salesforce Developer. I currently work for Philadelphia-based Clarivate Analytics as a Salesforce Administrator. I currently work for Clarivate Analytics, a Philadelphia-based company that sells research tools. I have a background in Business Administration, and received my MBA from Arcadia University in 2014.
